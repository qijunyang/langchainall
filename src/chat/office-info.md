# Acme Consulting — Office Information

## Office Hours
The office is open Monday through Friday, from 9:00 AM to 5:00 PM.
The office is closed on weekends (Saturday and Sunday).
The last client appointment of the day starts no later than 4:30 PM.

## Lunch and Breaks
Lunch break is from 12:30 PM to 1:30 PM each working day.
During lunch the front desk is unattended; phone calls go to voicemail.
Employees are entitled to two additional 15-minute breaks per day.

## Public Holidays and Closures
The office is closed on the following public holidays in 2026:
New Year's Day (January 1), Good Friday (April 3), Memorial Day (May 25),
Independence Day (July 3, observed), Labor Day (September 7),
Thanksgiving (November 26 and 27), and Christmas (December 24, 25, and 28).
The office also closes early, at 1:00 PM, on the last working day of each year.

## Location and Contact
The office is located at 100 Market Street, Suite 400, San Francisco, CA 94105.
Main phone: (415) 555-0142. General email: hello@acmeconsulting.example.
Visitor parking is available in the building garage on Mission Street.

## Remote Work Policy
Employees may work remotely up to two days per week, typically Monday and Friday.
Wednesday is a required in-office "collaboration day" for all teams.
Remote work requests for more than two days need manager approval in advance.

## Booking a Meeting
Clients can book meetings online at acmeconsulting.example/book or by phone.
Meeting rooms must be reserved at least 24 hours in advance.
Same-day meeting requests are accommodated only if a room is free.
